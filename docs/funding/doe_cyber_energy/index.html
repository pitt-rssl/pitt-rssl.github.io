Overview

The University of Pittsburgh Cyber Energy Center is dedicated to developing cybersecurity solutions and addressing the needs of the evolving cyber-energy workforce.

The RSSLab’s research within the center focuses on intrusion detection and tolerance using digital twins. We aim to develop intrusion-tolerant digital twins of key pieces of power grid infrastructure and create frameworks to shift control to the digital twins to enable seamlessly transitioning to more resilient system architectures without interruptions.
